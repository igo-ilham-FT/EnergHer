About EnergHer

EnergHer is a functional carbonated drink formulated for women navigating the changes of midlife. Pineapple & Mango flavoured, taurine-free and caffeine-free — energy that works with your body, not against it.

Every ingredient is chosen with purpose: from Lion's Mane for cognitive clarity to Vitamin B6 for sustained energy metabolism, and Calcium for everyday wellbeing.

Approved Health Claims

  • Vitamin B6 contributes to the reduction of tiredness and fatigue
  • Vitamin B6 contributes to normal energy-yielding metabolism
  • Calcium contributes to normal energy-yielding metabolism

Ingredients

Water, Erythritol, Calcium lactate, Lion's mane extract, Soy isoflavones, Mango flavoring, Citric acid, Pineapple flavoring, Bitterness blocker, Guar gum, Red clover extract, Stevia, Vitamin B6

Allergen information: Contains soy. Not suitable for individuals with a soy allergy.
