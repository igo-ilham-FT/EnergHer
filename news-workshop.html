Women's Health Workshop 2026

September 15, 2026

We are thrilled to announce the inaugural Women's Health Workshop 2026 — a full day dedicated to the science, stories, and strategies of women's midlife health. Hosted by the EnergHer team, this event brings together leading voices in nutrition, endocrinology, women's wellness, and functional beverage science.

What to Expect

The workshop is designed for women in their 40s, 50s, and beyond who want to take an informed, empowered approach to their health. Whether you're navigating perimenopause, deep into menopause, or simply planning ahead, this event is for you.

Talks will cover topics including: the science of hot flushes and how nutrition can help, strategies for managing brain fog and fatigue, the role of isoflavones and adaptogens in hormonal balance, and the growing body of research into women's specific nutritional needs in midlife.

"We built EnergHer because we believed that women in midlife deserved better than being told to just push through. This workshop is an extension of that belief."
— EnergHer Founding Team

Tastings & Community

Alongside the talks, attendees will have the opportunity to taste EnergHer and EnergHim, meet the team behind the formulations, and connect with a community of like-minded women who are redefining what it means to age with energy.

Light refreshments (including EnergHer, naturally) will be provided throughout the day. The event will be held in a venue accessible to all, and limited spots are available to ensure an intimate, high-quality experience.

Date & Location

Date: September 15, 2026
Time: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Location: Dublin, Ireland (venue TBC — registered attendees will receive full details)
Price: Free for EnergHer members · General admission details TBC
